Frequently asked questions
How is this different from asking ChatGPT?
Project Elevate uses 32 domain-specific expert profiles, pulls live data from FDA.gov, ClinicalTrials.gov, CDC, and PubMed at report time, and validates every claim with an independent AI critic. ChatGPT gives generic answers with no citations and can't access current regulatory data.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your profile page and you keep access until the end of your billing period.
What counts as a "report" on the Basic plan?
Each time you run a PI analysis, that counts as one report. Saved reports don't count — only new runs.
Do I need a credit card to try it?
You can create a free account and run 1 report without a credit card. A subscription is required for additional reports.
