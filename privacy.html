Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026 · Effective immediately

Project Elevate ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service at projectelevate1.netlify.app and related domains.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and (optionally) your institution or organization name. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google profile information.

Innovation descriptions: We collect the text you enter to describe health innovations when generating market analyses. This text is processed by our AI systems and may be retained to improve accuracy.

Usage data: We collect information about how you use the Service, including which features you access, analyses you run, and reports you save. This includes IP addresses, browser type, and access timestamps.

Early access form: If you complete our early access request form, we collect your name, email, institution, role, and any message you provide.

Payment information: We do not currently process payments directly. When billing is enabled, payment information will be processed by Stripe and subject to Stripe's privacy policy. We will not store full card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the Service, including generating market analyses
  • Send you account-related notifications (early access confirmations, important updates)
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of our AI models
  • Enforce usage limits and prevent abuse of our API resources
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Innovation Descriptions and AI Processing

The health innovation descriptions you submit are processed by:

  • Anthropic Claude API — for generating market analysis text. Anthropic's API processes your input to generate responses. Anthropic's data usage policies apply to this processing.
  • OpenAI API — for generating embeddings (numerical representations used for similarity search). OpenAI's data usage policies apply.

We recommend not including proprietary unpublished research details, trade secrets, or highly confidential IP in your submissions if you have concerns about third-party AI processing.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway (railway.app) in the United States. We implement reasonable technical security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission
  • Password hashing using bcrypt — we never store plaintext passwords
  • JWT token authentication with expiration
  • Rate limiting to prevent unauthorized bulk data extraction
  • Server-side quota enforcement to prevent API abuse

No security system is completely impenetrable. We will notify you of any confirmed data breach affecting your personal information within 72 hours of discovery.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with the following third-party services, each subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Railway (railway.app) — database and server hosting
  • Netlify (netlify.com) — frontend hosting and CDN
  • Anthropic (anthropic.com) — AI text generation (Claude)
  • OpenAI (openai.com) — AI embeddings
  • Amazon SES — transactional email delivery
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in via Google account

Public data sources (ClinicalTrials.gov, openFDA, NIH, WHO, CMS, SEC EDGAR, PubMed, PatentsView) are accessed read-only for analysis purposes. We do not share your personal data with these services.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies: only those necessary for authentication (storing your login session in localStorage). We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or third-party analytics services that collect personal data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data and saved reports for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., billing records).

Anonymous usage logs (for security and rate-limiting purposes) are retained for 90 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — request your saved reports in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@projectelevate.io.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
